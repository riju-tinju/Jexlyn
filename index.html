Frequently Asked Questions

Do you create static websites?

Indeed, a variety of website types including static, e-commerce, dynamic, and more can be created according to your specific needs and desired features.

What features are included in the admin page?

Admin page functionalities encompass content management, SEO capabilities, integration with Google Analytics API, interactive charts, advanced security measures, and the incorporation of innovative technologies or concepts like chatbots, ChatGPT, to elevate user experiences.

How much does your service cost?

Website pricing depends on the complexity and features of the website, and I provide tailored quotes based on individual project requirements.

How long does it take to develop a website?

The time required for website development varies depending on the website's complexity; for static websites, it may take around 4 to 7 days, while dynamic websites can take approximately 15 to 30 days.
